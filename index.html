Ranking the top 6 Ohio Downtowns

6. Toledo: Kind of looks like a factory town, not a very high- tech place to live. Very depressing. 2/10
5. Dayton: Not much going on, better than Toldeo, but still lacks modernity. 4/10
4: Akron: Very lush, lots of nature, but looks like a college campus, not very interesting to look at. 5/10
3. Cleveland: Getting to the better ones, lots of variety but there are better ones out there. 7/10
2. Cincinnati: Lots going on. The connection with Kentucky with the bridge *Chefs Kiss*. 9/10
1. Columbus: Perfect balance with greenery and buildings, not very overwhelming, still looks amazing. 10/10
